General Rules

  1. A team should consist of 10 members.
  2. Any number of teams per college.
  3. No registration fee.
  4. Participants are requested to carry laptops with internet connection.
  5. Possession of college ID card and permission letter is mandatory.
  6. If a team is requesting for accomodation,the team must submit the print out copy of the submitted registration form.
  7. Duly filled registration form should reach us on or before March 14 2015
  8. Teams have to report at the reception desk by 8 a.m on the first day of the Semaphore’15.
  9. Participating team should not reveal their college identity during their events (no college t-shirt or college logos). All colleges participating should abide by the rules and regulations specified in the brochure. Discipline should be strictly maintained. Any indisciplinary act will lead to the disqualification of the college team or any individual from the entire event with respect to the events.
  10. Decision of the judges will be final.
  11. Accommodation facilities can be provided for 48 hours on prior notification.
  12. The event management committee reserves the right to modify the rules, regulations and time schedule, if necessary.
  13. Switching of candidates once the event start off will not be entertained.
  14. Participation in all the events is MANDATORY for the overall championship.
  15. All Participants can Participate in General Events
